ABOUT ME

I am currently an Associate Professor at the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence, Southwest Jiaotong University, China. My research interests are in multimodal deep learning, transfer learning and their applications in computer vision, natural language processing and social network analysis.

I have openings for self-motivated undergraduate students, master students and PhD students (co-supervised). Feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research area.

RESEARCH INTERESTS

  • Multimodal Deep Learning: multimodal fusion, multimodal recognition
  • Transfer Learning: domain adaptation, zero-shot leaning, few-shot learning
  • Computer Vision: semantic segmentation, video understanding, medical imaging analysis
  • Natural Language Processing: sentiment analysis, aspect term extraction
  • Data Mining: social media analysis, sentiment analysis
